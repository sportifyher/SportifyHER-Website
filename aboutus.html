About Us

Get To Know Us

Our Mission

At SportifyHer, our mission is to empower girls and women in sports by breaking down barriers, providing critical funding, and creating equal opportunities for growth, visibility, and success. We’re here to make sure female athletes have the resources, support, and recognition they deserve

Our Vision

We envision a world where women’s sports are fully valued, celebrated, and supported—where every girl, regardless of background, has the chance to lead, compete, and thrive. SportifyHer is building that future by shifting culture, driving equity, and elevating the next generation of game-changers

Our Team

Behind SportifyHer is a passionate, purpose-driven team of athletes, advocates, creatives, and leaders—united by a belief in the power of sport to transform lives. Together, we’re reimagining what’s possible for women in sport and making that vision a reality

What We Do

SportifyHer funds and supports girls and women in sports by providing financial aid, resources, and visibility. We help athletes access what they need to train, compete, and succeed—while building a more equal and empowered future for women in sport through advocacy, storytelling, and community
